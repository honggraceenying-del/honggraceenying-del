Data Analytics, Infrastructure & SAP by PPG

Industry Talk 1 | Talk Poster regards of PPG

December 04, 2025 Grace Hong

It is an excellent, inspiring study through the talk from PPG about Data Analytics, SAP COE, and Global Infrastructure teams collaborating to support business goals. This is my first time understanding data analytics COE, global infrastructure & cloud, SAP center of excellence, and career & skills. Although the talk mainly emphasizes data that is more regarding to Data Engineering students, as a Graphic and Multimedia Software student, I learnt that instead of relying on a user's local GPU, software can also offload intensive tasks such as 3D ray tracing or AI upscaling to high-performance cloud clusters regarding to cloud rendering & processing. Besides, data analytics skills will help me study user behavior and conduct trend analysis, which I can use to create applications and games that are more valuable in the market.

A Visit to Johor Port

Industrial Visit 2 | Explore IT in Johor Port

January 08, 2026 Grace Hong

I can't believe we had the chance to visit Johor Port in Pasir Gudang! I'm so grateful to our lecturer and Johor Port for giving us a glimpse into the backend of their ICT operations. I learned about the four main pillars that support their system: Infrastructure, Business Applications, Project & Governance and Admin & Helpdesk. It was fascinating to see the strategic technology they use, such as JPBi, CATOS, and MSIS. This was my first time being introduced to these systems. Although I'm still processing everything I saw, I'm excited to continue discovering how they work in the future.
